The Best Movies According to Aleks

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Lego Batman Movie

A very comedic movie that directly represents me.
Batman's character is setup to build character growth and develop
into someone who's not as self-centered as he was at the beginning of the movie.
Him being afraid of letting people get close to him and then being forced to
accept them as his own family was a huge indicator of substantial growth.

Whiplash

Whiplash is an absolute masterpiece of a movie and I could
use this entire page just talking about it but it's amazing! The acting alone
was beyond subpar and the characters interactions with each other made it that much better!

A Silent Voice

Quite honestly this is another movie that directly represents me
and it was such a heartwarming and carefully constructed anime love story.
